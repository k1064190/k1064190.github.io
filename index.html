About Me

I am a passionate AI researcher currently pursuing my Master's degree at Yonsei University. As a second-year graduate student, my research focuses on the intersection of multimodal large language models (LLMs) and speech processing technologies. I am particularly interested in enhancing LLM reasoning capabilities, developing advanced automatic speech recognition (ASR) and text-to-speech (TTS) systems, and pioneering the integration of speech and language models for real-time applications.

My work aims to bridge the gap between different modalities in AI, creating systems that can understand and process information as naturally as humans do. I am dedicated to advancing these technologies through innovative research and collaboration with fellow researchers in the field.
